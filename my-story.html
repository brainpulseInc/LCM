Successful in Business
But Unfulfilled in Life?
I Know That Feeling.

My Story

Having a successful career and a hefty bank account does not always make you happy. I learned that the hard way.

And maybe you are starting to realize this as well. If so, I have a story that might help...

Not so long ago, I was an overachiever on the brink of burnout. I had a thriving business and all of the appearances of having achieved ultimate success. But appearances can be, and often are, deceiving. That is why I am here to help you.

In reality, I was overwhelmed, stressed out and unfulfilled. If that is where you are right now, I know how you feel.

But my own story taught me something invaluable: dramatic transformation is possible.

When Success Doesn’t Equal Happiness

I was born in Morocco and quickly learned the value of money. It is a lesson that has served me well throughout my life.

After leaving Morocco, I worked tirelessly across North America, China and Europe in a range of careers in software development, real estate, executive consulting, and entrepreneurship.

The global exposure, along with living and working in many foreign cultures, taught me the uniqueness and importance of cultural sensitivity when it comes to business and personal affairs.

Language barriers, and dealing with them as a professional immigrant, taught me the importance of patience, understanding, and how to present concepts in a unique way for whomever I dealt with. I was incredibly successful in my professional realm and established long­lasting business relationships across the globe.

My hard work paid off. By the time I turned 30, I had built up to a $1 million portfolio. I helped multinational entrepreneurs of many different walks of life grow their business. I became a consultant to several Fortune 500 companies.

On the surface, I looked like I had it all. But something was just not right.

No matter how much professional praise I received or money I made, I did not feel satisfied. I became increasingly frustrated at not being able to scale up my own business and could not see a way out.

Life was not working out as I wanted it to. I was tired. I was frustrated. I felt hopeless. Something had to change, and I realized the answer to the question of WHAT was within me.

A Search For Help

If I was ever going to be successful and happy, I knew I could not do it alone. That is when I sought the help of multiple life coaches, mentors, meditation masters and, ultimately, created the balanced life I have today.

But then I realized something: countless other people must be facing the same problems I had, unsure where to turn next.

That is what led me to set up Life Coach Montreal.

Today, I use my unique experience, business background and expertize in the technology sector to help businesses and individuals reach their full potential.

Here is How My Experience Can Help You:

  • I have helped entrepreneurs both big and small expand their business.
    I have lived in three different continents, 5 different countries, and I am multi­lingual. Through my international and cross­cultural experience, coupled with my vast business knowledge and exposure, I can help streamline your operation, overcome stumbling blocks and show you how to cultivate a more balanced life.
  • I have worked as an executive with leading Fortune 500 companies.
    When it comes to transforming your business, I bring a wealth of experience derived from working in top level position with some of the world’s most prominent organizations, including Univserve, Ubisoft and Desjardins.
  • I have an intimate knowledge of the technology/startup sector.
    Over the past 10 years, I have spent time as a software engineer and IT consultant. That means I am a life coach who understands your industry and knows what kind of specific advice will make the biggest difference.

I would love to talk to you to find out if Life Coaching is right for you. My job is to help you achieve a more meaningful Work/Life balance.
